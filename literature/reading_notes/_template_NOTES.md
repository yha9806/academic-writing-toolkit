## Source format examples

Use the one matching your `CLAUDE.md` `Citation style:`. The active style decides the `**Source**:` line shape above. `/audit` Tier 0 lints the Source line against the active style's pattern.

```
# harvard
**Source**: Smith, J. (2024) Title in sentence case. *Publisher*.

# apa
**Source**: Smith, J. (2024). Title in sentence case. *Publisher*. https://doi.org/...

# chicago-author-date
**Source**: Smith, John. 2024. *Title in Title Case*. Publisher.

# mla
**Source**: Smith, John. *Title in Title Case*. Publisher, 2024.

# ieee
**Source**: [1] J. Smith, "Title in title case," *Journal*, vol. 12, no. 3, pp. 45-67, 2024.

# vancouver
**Source**: 1. Smith J. Title in sentence case. Journal. 2024;12(3):45-67.

# gb-t-7714-2015
**Source**: Smith J. Title in sentence case[J]. *Journal*, 2024, 12(3): 45-67.
```

For multi-author (≥3 authors), include the full list per your declared style; `/audit` handles `et al.` rendering on the in-text side per style rules.
