# Academic Writing Toolkit v0.5.0 Release Readiness

`v0.5.0` is the first candidate centred explicitly on recovering and governing
the author's research project before revising prose.

## Release Scope

- adds `/academic-writing-assistant` as the project-level front door;
- locks the field gap, exact paper contribution, empirical finding, headline
  claim, licensed scope, and explicitly unlicensed claims;
- links the assistant and argument-governance `intent_id` to the durable
  project-intent, manuscript-contract, and global-audit layer;
- separates gaps, data, results, claims, contributions, innovation evidence,
  and limiting evidence through the strict relation profile;
- reviews contribution focus without total-score selection or automatic
  manuscript reframing;
- stops after three failed revisions and requires diagnosis plus an author
  decision before another edit;
- preserves factual project-session summaries;
- expands the package to 21 Skills;
- adds a machine-checkable OpenAI skills-only submission packet with exactly
  five positive and three negative reviewer cases;
- replaces outdated listing screenshots with reproducible v0.5.0 workflow
  visuals.

## Reviewer Packet

Canonical file:

`submission/openai/skills-only-submission.json`

It contains:

- listing copy, support/privacy/terms URLs, region owner gate, and release notes;
- four documented policy boundaries;
- publisher-identity, Apps Management Write, submit, and publish owner gates;
- five positive cases covering project intent, argument levels, evidence
  licence, contribution focus, and three-revision escalation;
- three negative cases covering unrelated requests, fabricated evidence, and
  author-gate bypass.

Static validation does not replace forward-testing. Run all eight prompts
against the final plugin ZIP and record the tester, date, ZIP SHA-256, and
outcome before portal submission.

## Human Gates

Release and OpenAI submission remain blocked until the maintainer confirms:

- the PR is merged and its Linux CI is green;
- the tag points to the verified `main` commit;
- the eight reviewer prompts pass against the final ZIP;
- the publisher identity is verified and matches the listing;
- the account has Apps Management Write;
- region and policy fields are confirmed;
- the exact tested ZIP is uploaded;
- the owner manually submits and, after approval, publishes.

Scientific truth, contribution importance, venue fit, and final author intent
remain human scholarly judgements.
