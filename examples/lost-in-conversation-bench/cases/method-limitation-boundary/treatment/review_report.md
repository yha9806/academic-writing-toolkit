# Treatment Review

## Workflow

Treatment converts the multi-turn requirements into a spine card and edit contract before revising the subsection. The edited section is then checked through a drift audit.

## Metric Review

| Metric | Review |
| --- | --- |
| Spine preservation | Strong. The subsection remains a methods limitation rather than a results claim or benchmark claim. |
| Claim drift | Low and audited. The audit records `changed_claims=none`. |
| Evidence boundary | Strong. The edit states that three sessions support local analytic visibility rather than representative generalisation. |
| Scope discipline | Strong. Pattern reporting remains assigned to the next chapter. |
| Author control recovery | Strong. The author can inspect the spine card, applied contract, edited section, and audit row before accepting the change. |

## Control Finding

The treatment output is not merely smoother prose. It makes the protected limitation visible before editing and confirms after editing that the claim boundary remains intact.
